Help Center

How to Use the App

To use the Sign Language Recognition app, follow these simple steps:

  • Upload a Video: Navigate to the Upload Video page, select a video file of sign language gestures, and click "Upload and Decode."
  • Use Webcam: Go to the Real-Time Recognition page to start recognizing sign language gestures using your webcam.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, try the following solutions:

  • Video Not Uploading: Ensure your video file is in a supported format (e.g., MP4, AVI) and the file size is reasonable.
  • Webcam Not Working: Make sure your browser has permission to access your webcam. You may need to enable webcam access in your browser's settings.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team at support@signlanguagerecognition.com.
